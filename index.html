About Joey

Meet Joey Santes.
Built on hard work.
Driven by relationships.

I'm Joey Santes, a commercial painting estimator and project manager with over a decade of hands-on experience delivering large-scale painting projects on time, on budget, and to spec. My work spans office buildings, retail centers, industrial facilities, healthcare campuses, and municipal structures.

I got into this field because I believe great results come from precision and teamwork, building on relationships from the bid invite, to the site, and in the execution. I believe every problem can be solved with the right team around you, a clear plan, and the end result always in mind.

Whether I am working as an estimator, consultant, or PM for a general contractor or developer, my commitment is the same — doing the work diligently, clear communication, meticulous documentation, and providing a finished product that speaks for itself.

What I Offer

Whether you need a detail-driven PM on your team or an experienced estimator to win more work — here's how I can help.

01
Project Management

End-to-end oversight of commercial painting projects — from pre-construction coordination to punch-list closeout. I keep crews on schedule, subs accountable, and clients informed at every phase.

02
Estimating & Bidding

Accurate, competitive bids built from thorough takeoffs and real material and labor data. I prepare proposals that win work without leaving money on the table or setting unrealistic expectations.

03
Site Coordination

On-site leadership and subcontractor management that keeps painting scopes running clean alongside other trades. I resolve conflicts fast and protect your schedule.

04
Specification Review

Deep-dive review of painting specs, submittals, and product data sheets to ensure the right systems are specified, approved, and installed correctly the first time.

05
Budget & Cost Control

Proactive tracking of project costs against budget — identifying variances early and implementing corrective action before small overruns become real problems.

06
Consulting

Need a second set of eyes on a bid, a project plan, or a scope of work? I'm available for short-term consulting engagements for painting contractors and general contractors alike.
