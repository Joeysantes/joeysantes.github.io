What I Offer

Whether you need detail-driven estimating, bidding, prequalification, consulting, or project management help — here is how I can help.

01
Estimating

Accurate, competitive estimates built from thorough takeoffs and real material and labor data. I prepare proposals that are tight, defensible, and built to win work without leaving money on the table or setting unrealistic expectations.

02
Bidding Process & Follow-Up

From the initial bid invite through updated plan sets, RFI responses, and leveling — I manage the full bid cycle. I track addenda, follow up with the GC or owner, and stay engaged through the award so nothing falls through the cracks.

03
Prequalification

I handle the full prequalification process — assembling documentation, completing GC and owner preq packages, and positioning your company to get on the right bid lists and stay there.

04
Project Management

End-to-end oversight of commercial painting projects — from pre-construction coordination to punch-list closeout. I keep crews on schedule, subs accountable, and clients informed at every phase.

05
Site Coordination

On-site leadership and subcontractor management that keeps painting scopes running clean alongside other trades. I resolve conflicts fast and protect your schedule.

06
Consulting

Need a second set of eyes on a bid, a project plan, or a scope of work? I'm available for short-term consulting engagements for painting contractors and general contractors alike.

About Joey

Meet Joey Santes.
Built on hard work.
Driven by relationships.

I'm Joey Santes, a commercial painting estimator and project manager with over 18 years of hands-on experience delivering small, mid-size, and large-scale painting projects on time, on budget, and to spec. My work spans office buildings, retail centers, industrial facilities, healthcare campuses, and municipal structures.

I got into this field because I believe great results come from precision and teamwork, building on relationships from the bid invite, to the site, and in the execution. I believe every problem can be solved with the right team around you, a clear plan, and the end result always in mind.

Whether I am working as an estimator, consultant, or PM for a general contractor or developer, my commitment is the same — doing the work diligently, clear communication, meticulous documentation, and providing a finished product that speaks for itself.
